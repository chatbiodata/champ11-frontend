📲 How Notifications Work
🔔 Notification 1 — XI Confirmed
Fires at: ~7:07 PM (evening) / ~3:06 PM (afternoon)
Title: "🔥 RCB vs SRH — Dhurandhar Playing 11 LIVE!"
Body: "Ladies & Gentlemen... Get Ready 🔥 AI team READY! 23 min to lock!"
Trigger: Teams saved to Firebase after XI fetch
⏱ Notification 2 — 15 Min Reminder
Fires at: 7:15 PM (evening) / 3:15 PM (afternoon)
Title: "⏱ 15 Min Left — RCB vs SRH! 🏏"
Body: "FINAL CALL! Lock AI Dhurandhar Playing 11 NOW!"
Trigger: Cron at 13:45 / 09:45 UTC
Fallback (no VAPID): Frontend polls /api/matches/today-status every 2 min.
When xiConfirmed: true detected → bell shakes + in-app toast fires automatically.
To enable true push: Add VAPID_PUBLIC_KEY + VAPID_PRIVATE_KEY to Railway env vars.

📋 Daily Curl Commands
Copy & Run in WSL
Run these commands daily in WSL to keep AI training updated
1. Match Sync (run once or when new season)
Syncs IPL 2025 schedule from CricAPI to Firebase
2. Daily Backtest Fetch (run daily, 10 days)
Fetches 15 historical match scorecards per day
3. Seed Players (run once)
Seeds 101 IPL players with Dream11 credit data
4. Train AI (run after all scorecards fetched)
Build player intel + run backtest + update AI weights
5. Check Status
View AI training progress and accuracy
6. Reset Empty Scorecards
Deletes empty records so they can be re-fetched
